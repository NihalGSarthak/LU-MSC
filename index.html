About the Council

The Meritorious Student Council (MSC) serves as the vital bridge between the student body and the University administration. We are dedicated to fostering an environment of excellence through institutional development, Research & Development (R&D) initiatives, and a robust student-centric approach. Our primary objective is to streamline communication and provide an effective grievance redressal system to ensure the welfare of every student at the University of Lucknow.
